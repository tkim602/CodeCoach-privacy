Privacy Policy

CodeCoach — Chrome Extension
Last updated: 2026-06-08

CodeCoach is a Chrome extension that provides progressive AI hints, code analysis, debugging guidance, local study notes, and weakness tracking on supported coding-practice pages such as LeetCode and Programmers.

Summary

Information stored locally in your browser

AI requests are sent directly from your browser to OpenAI using your own API key. The CodeCoach developer does not receive, proxy, store, or log the content of your OpenAI requests.
